Planets in Our Solar System

  • Mercury - The smallest and closest planet to the Sun.
  • Venus - The hottest planet in the solar system.
  • Earth - Our home, the only known planet with life.
  • Mars - The red planet, a candidate for future colonization.
  • Jupiter - The largest planet with a Great Red Spot.
  • Saturn - Famous for its beautiful rings.
  • Uranus - A tilted planet with a bluish color.
  • Neptune - The farthest known planet, deep blue in color.
